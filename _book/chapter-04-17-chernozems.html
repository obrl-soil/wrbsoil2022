21  Chernozems

Other soils having:

  1. a chernic horizon; and
  2. starting ≤ 50 cm below the lower limit of the mollic1 horizon and, if present, above a petrocalcic horizon, a layer with protocalcic properties, ≥ 5 cm thick, or a calcic horizon; and
  3. a base saturation (by 1 M NH4OAc, pH 7)2 of ≥ 50% from the mineral soil surface to the layer with protocalcic properties or to the calcic horizon, throughout.
