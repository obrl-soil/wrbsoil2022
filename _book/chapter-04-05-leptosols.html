Leptosols

Modified

October 8, 2022

Other soils having:

  1. one of the following:
    1. continuous rock starting ≤ 25 cm from the soil surface; or
    2. < 20% (by volume, related to the whole soil) fine earth1, averaged over a depth of 75 cm from the soil surface or to continuous rock, whichever is shallower; and
  2. no chernic, duric, petrocalcic, petroduric, petrogypsic, petroplinthic, pisoplinthic or spodic horizon.
